Topic 1: Socrates

Reading

(This section lists required readings and where to find them. For due dates, see the schedule page.)

  1. Plato, Apology, first part. Translated by GMA Grube, in Five Dialogues 2nd ed., pages 21–28. Or, see the PDF under Course Documents on Blackboard for a stopgap measure while you’re acquiring the books.
  2. Plato, Crito, translated by GMA Grube, in Five Dialogues 2nd ed. (12 pages total)
  3. Plato, Hippias Major in Socrates and the Sophists, tr. Joe Sachs
  4. Plato, Euthydemus in Socrates and the Sophists, tr. Joe Sachs
  5. Plato, Meno, translated by GMA Grube, in Five Dialogues 2nd ed.
